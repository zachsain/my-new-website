List of my life's passions:

MUSIC

My first passion in life was music. Playing instruments from a very young age, I grew a deep love for the study of harmony and melody. I started off playing drums and trombone, then transitioned to guitar, then bass, and then keyboard. But my favorite part about creating music was song writing. After graduating highschool I studied Jazz Guitar at University of Northern Colorado, then Jazz Piano at Loyola University New Orleans. After dropping out to play professionally a few years, I would go back to finish my degree at a small conservatory in Los Angeles called Los Angeles College of Music, where I studied Audio Engineering and Music Production.

TECHNOLOGY

During my time between playing music professionally and going back to school to finish my degree I developed a big passion for technology and tech-startups. I loved the idea that building and being involved in innovate technology has the power to impact society in a very profound way. It was the first time I found something I loved just as much as music. I found that it required creativity and problem solving skills the same way that writing a great song would. I also loved the people that I worked with. It's been the only job I had where I didn't feel like I was actually going to work.
